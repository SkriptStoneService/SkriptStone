Alchemy SMP

$10.00
Core Concept

Survival system where your limited to how many potions you can hold. Tied to "Alchemy" levels. Reach zero, and you are death banned.

Mechanics
  • Start with 5 potion slots
  • Kill = +1 Alchemy slot
  • Death = -1 Alchemy slot
  • Max limit: 13 slots
System Commands
/Alchemysmp getitem [item] Admin: Spawn Alchemy, Revive, DragonSlayer, or PotionLimiter
/Alchemy [get/set] [player] Admin: View or manually set a player's slot count
/Alchemy Check your current slot limit
/withdraw [amount] Convert levels into items
/Alchemy-bans View death-banned players
