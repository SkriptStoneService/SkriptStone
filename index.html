Alchemy SMP

Core Concept

A survival system where your life and inventory capacity are tied together by your "Alchemy" level. Reach zero, and you are death banned.

Mechanics
  • Start with 5 potion slots
  • Kill = +1 Alchemy slot
  • Death = -1 Alchemy slot
  • Max limit: 13 slots
System Commands
/Alchemy Check your current slot limit
/withdraw [amount] Convert Alchemy levels into physical items
/Alchemy-bans View a list of currently death-banned players
/Alchemy get [player] Admin: View player stats
/Alchemy set [player] Admin: Adjust player stats
/Alchemysmp getitem Admin: Spawn mythic's, revives/alchemy items
